🔐 PaperTrail – Privacy Policy
Effective date: April 1, 2026
Publisher: Kelci Griffin

1. Overview
PaperTrail is a local‑first desktop application.
Your privacy is a core design principle.
PaperTrail does not collect, transmit, or store data on external servers.

2. Data Collection
PaperTrail:

❌ Does not collect personal data
❌ Does not use analytics
❌ Does not use telemetry
❌ Does not collect crash reports
❌ Does not communicate with remote servers

No accounts, logins, or identifiers are required.

3. Local Data Storage
All application data (tasks, projects, notes, metrics, settings) is stored entirely on your device using local storage (IndexedDB).
Kelci Griffin has no access to your data.

4. Export and Import

Data export and import are user‑initiated only
Exported files are stored where you choose
PaperTrail never uploads exported data automatically


5. Notifications
PaperTrail may display local system notifications generated entirely on your device.
No remote messaging or push notification services are used.

6. Third‑Party Services
PaperTrail does not integrate with third‑party APIs or cloud providers.

7. Children’s Privacy
PaperTrail is intended for users 13 years of age or older.
No personal data is knowingly collected from minors.

8. Future Changes
If PaperTrail’s data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and made available through official release channels.

9. Contact
For privacy‑related questions or concerns, please open an issue at:
👉 https://github.com/kelcigriffin/PaperTrail/issues
